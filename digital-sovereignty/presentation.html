Assessment Criteria

Five dimensions:

  • ⚖️ Human Rights — Military? Surveillance?
  • 🔒 Data Sovereignty — CLOUD Act?
  • 🤖 AI Ethics — Training data? Bias?
  • 🌱 Environmental — Carbon footprint?
  • 🔓 Lock-in — Exit costs?
